Potato Soup Recipe

I have made this potato soup for many whom have given it this title. It takes a bit of effort but is well worth it. Please note: for those who do not wish to use bacon, substitute 1/4 cup melted butter for the bacon grease and continue with the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon,chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 8 potatoes
  • 4 cups chicken stalk
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream

Steps

  1. gather all ingredients
  2. place bacon in dutch oven
  3. cook celery and onion
  4. add cubed potatoes
  5. whisk in flour and heavy cream and cilantro
  6. bring cream mixture to a boil mix in potatoes
  7. transfer about 1/2 of the soup to a blender adjust seasonings
  8. enjoy
