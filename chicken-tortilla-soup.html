Chicken Tortilla Soup

  • 1(4 1/2 pound)whole chicken
  • 3 white onions, halved,divided
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, rinsed well
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
  • 2 dried chili pepper pods, seeded
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

    • Steps

    1. Combine chicken, 1 onion, stems from the cilantro bunch
    2. Meanwhile make the tortilla strips preheat oven 350 degrees F line a baking sheet with a silicone mat paper
    3. bake in preheated oven until golden brown
    4. let chicken rest cool enough to pull off
    5. add cumin, shredded chicken and juice from the lime
